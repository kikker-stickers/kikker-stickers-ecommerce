About Kikker Stickers

About Us

Kikker stickers is a small sticker business located in Jenison, Michigan. It was started by Brian DeVries and Andrew VanDyke to make ordering bulk stickers online easy. Now you don't have to do the image formatting, we will convert any image into a printable format for you and send a proof before printing.

What does "kikker" mean?

Brian was learning to speak Dutch when the frog was chosen as the mascot. His wife Hannah looked up "frog" in Dutch and found out it was "kikker". "Kikker Stickers" had a nice ring to it and the brand was born!
